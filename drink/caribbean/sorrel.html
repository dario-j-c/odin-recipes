Sorrel

Description

Sorrel ws a spiced floral drink whose dark red or burgundy colour appropriately matches the season in which its most consumed

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried sorrel buds
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1½ cup grated ginger
  • 1 lime
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1½ cup grated orange zest (optional)
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water
  2. Place 1 cup of sugar in a jug and add boiling water
  3. Stir until the sugar dissolves
  4. In a large bowl, place the following
    • Dried sorrel buds
    • Grated ginger
    • Juice of one lime
    • Cinnamon stick
  5. Gently pour boiled water and sugar mixture into the bowl.
  6. Steep for approx. 45 minutes
  7. Refrigerate for 24-48 hrs (Optional)
  8. Strain when ready to serve
  9. Serve as desired, with or without ice
